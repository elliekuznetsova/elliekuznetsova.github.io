Elizaveta “Ellie” Kuznetsova

I’m currently an M.S. student in Computational Social Science at UC San Diego, where I get to combine my love for language, data, and understanding how people communicate. Before starting this program, I spent several years teaching languages and working closely with multilingual learners. Along the way, I discovered my interests in building datasets, data analyses, and exploring how quantitative methods can reveal patterns in human language, which led me to computational work. These days, I’m especially excited about anything that brings language and technology together. My interests include bilingualism, code-switching, natural language processing, corpus linguistics, cognitive linguistics, psycholinguistics, and second language acquisition.
