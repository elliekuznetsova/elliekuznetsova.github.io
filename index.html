About me

I’m an M.S. student in Computational Social Science at UC San Diego, combining my interests in language, data, and human communication. My research focuses on bilingualism, code-switching, natural language processing, corpus linguistics, cognitive linguistics, psycholinguistics, and second language acquisition. You can reach me at ekuznetsova at ucsd dot edu. Some of my work can also be found on my GitHub.
